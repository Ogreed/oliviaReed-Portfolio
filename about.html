About Me


     Hey there! My name is Olivia Reed and those who know me would describe me as an ambitious, motivated, hard-working, young lady. Some fun facts about me: I love animals (especially cats), my favorite Adobe software to use is Adobe Illustrator; however, I have recently started taking some classes and earning several certifications for Adobe XD and I have found it's interface to be amazing for wireframing which is one of my favorite things to do, and I am looking forward to using it for prototyping as well. Two sayings that I apply to everything that I do are as follows: "hard work will always pay off" and "a job half done is a job not done at all" and anyone who meets me, and especially those who have worked or volunteered with me can testify that that is true. Finally, although I am a web designer, I also love graphic design and therefore I do showcase some of my graphic design projects that I have completed in my My Portfolio along with my web design projects. If you are interested in any of my services, either my web or graphic design feel free to reach out to me at OliviaGReed03@gmail.com and check out my resume to learn more about me, my skills, and my work experience.
