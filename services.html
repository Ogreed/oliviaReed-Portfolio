Responsive Web Design

     Web design is not only my passion, but also what my degree is in. I love getting to take a concept from traditional wireframe, to digital wireframe, to mockup, to prototype, to finally (my favorite part) creating the code and turning a concept…a dream…into a true reality. In this digital age having a fully functional, well working, responsively designed website is a non-negotiable and designing these sites for individuals or businesses will forever be my passion.
